Some servers place bans that require a "voucher" of good behavior from another server to be appealed.

This page lists servers that participate in the voucher system in some way. Servers that **place** voucher bans are ones which sometimes require a banned player to provide a voucher for their appeal to be considered. Servers that **offer** vouchers are ones which are sometimes willing to provide their players with vouchers. Servers that **accept** vouchers are ones which place voucher bans or would take a voucher into consideration, even if they do not require it for any bans.

## Note for players
The table below may be inaccurate or out of date. Servers might change practices or policies before you are able to get a voucher. Servers may have vastly different requirements for getting a voucher. Only the servers themselves can provide you with up to date information on their current practices.
